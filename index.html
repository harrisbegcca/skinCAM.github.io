Privacy Policy

Privacy:
The images analyzed are stored in a cloud database known as “Firebase,” and accessed through an intermediate Python script to assess in a machine learning algorithm. We promise that our app will not collect user data and pictures for self benefit or personal matters; your privacy matters to us.

App Accuracy:
The app is not proven to work 100% of the time, thus you must take the results of each test lightly, as the model may have picked up background noise contributing to its false analysis of the image itself.
